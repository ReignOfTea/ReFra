What's new in 5.0.1:

• More image formats — View PSD, JPEG 2000, TIFF, and SVG files, with crisp pinch-to-zoom for each
• Faster HEIC/AVIF — Hardware-accelerated decoding with fixed AVIF color artifacts
• Use system font — New display option to switch the app over to your device's system font
• High-quality JXL zoom — Sharp JPEG XL zoom in the viewer via a dedicated region decoder

Improvements:
• Video now plays only through the media volume stream. 'Request audio focus' option is removed.
• Dismiss the What's New card without having to open it

Bug fixes:
• Fixed editor markup text gestures (rotate handle, stay-in-bounds) and crop box interactions
• Fixed timeline scroll position not being preserved when returning from the viewer
• Fixed settings scroll position resetting when opening a detail page
• Fixed album cover not matching the first item shown inside the album
• Fixed broken viewer exit animations from shared-element transitions
• Fixed black screen in the standalone viewer after deleting the current photo
• Fixed a crash on launch when MediaStore returned null columns
• Fixed video zoom on forced rotation and made pinch-to-zoom feel natural
• Fixed the back gesture being ignored on videos when controls were hidden
• Fixed the video info section closing when auto-hide fired
• Fixed panorama compass and gyro axes on rotation
• Fixed text disappearing and the cursor jumping in date format fields
• Fixed the scrubber resetting to its default value near zero
• Fixed home screen widgets when the cached bitmap was missing
• Fixed Facebook export photos showing future dates in the timeline
• Fixed the search bar placeholder overflowing to a second line
• Removed the erroneous 'Copied to clipboard' toast on Android 13+
• Updated Simplified Chinese translations
