What's new in 4.2.3:

• Timeline Filter — Filter the timeline by media type, favorites, year, or album directly from the search bar
• Animated Format Support — Play animated AVIF, JXL, and APNG images alongside GIF
• Video Subtitles — Select embedded or external subtitle tracks during video playback with a new bottom-sheet manager
• Video Pinch-to-Zoom — Pinch-to-zoom and double-tap-to-zoom during video playback
• Search Descriptions & Metadata — Search now matches image descriptions, EXIF metadata, and other embedded text fields
• Album Picker Search — Search bar in the copy/move album picker for quick album lookup
• Redesigned Trash & Favorites — Refreshed layout, improved visual hierarchy, and smoother animations

UX improvements:
• Filter moved to inline search bar with settings toggle
• Dismissible Donate/GitHub banners in Settings
• Bottom sheet confirmations replace dismiss X button
• Optimistic UI feedback when trashing images

Bug fixes:
• Fixed drag-selection tracking not initializing without prior selection
• Fixed keyboard overlapping metadata edit bottom sheet
• Preserved original DATE_TAKEN and DATE_ADDED when overriding edited photos
