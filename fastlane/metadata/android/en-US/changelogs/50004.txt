What's new in 5.0.0:

• Cloud media & Immich — Connect your self-hosted server and browse your remote library in a unified timeline with local/remote dedup, full-quality streaming, EXIF, server albums, people, map, and CLIP smart search
• Cloud photo backup — Back up selected albums with deduplication and Wi-Fi/cellular controls
• Backup & restore wizard — Export/import settings, favorites, cloud configs, and vaults, with optional password encryption
• New editor effects — Posterize, Edges, Borders, Hue, black & white, and a one-tap Negative preset
• Flexible date grouping — Group media by day, month, or year, configured per screen
• Album sections — Collapsible Common, Apps, and Other album sections
• Save motion photo as video — Export the embedded clip as a standalone MP4
• Android Advanced Protection — Auto-hardens decoding and metadata isolation when enabled (Android 16+)
• Location detail sheet — Map preview with quick open actions

Performance:
• Smoother timeline scrolling with optimized grid rendering and paused loading while fast-scrolling
• New Sketch-based viewer image pipeline with grid-click prefetching
• Faster startup and reduced bottom-sheet blur lag

Bug fixes:
