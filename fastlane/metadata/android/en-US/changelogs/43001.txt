What's new in 4.3.0:

• Story Cards — Swipeable photo stories above the timeline with auto-advancing viewer, surfacing highlights from recent photos, albums, categories, locations, and favorites
• Redesigned Video Controls — Animated more-options popup replaces the tall stack of video control buttons
• Video Auto-Contrast — Adaptive colors now extend to the video options popup

Performance:
• Cold startup optimized from ~900 ms to ~350 ms with lazy keystore caching and deferred initialization

Improvements:
• Improved shared element transitions with single stable registration and Long-based keys
• Loading shimmer during timeline startup instead of blank screen

Bug fixes:
• Fixed move/copy album access with MANAGE_MEDIA permission
• Fixed screen rotation not resetting when leaving video player
• Fixed status bar sync with followTheme logic in media viewer
• Fixed ClassCastException after unlock in StandaloneActivity
• Fixed grid realignment glitch on back navigation
• Fixed empty-media padding with above-grid content
